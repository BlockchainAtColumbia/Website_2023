LionDAO

B@C launched LionDAO in hopes of expanding the scope of our opportunities for members and allow for members and alumni to engage with the organization in a crypto-native way.

Joining LionDAO:

B@C issues its members digital tokens known as LION. These LION are used for things like voting on some of B@C's decisions. After you have completed your initial session, we will send you some tokens so you can be part of our DAO.
